About

I am a freelance journalist based in New York City, focusing on technology, culture, and the places where they collide. My work has appeared in The Atlantic, Wired, and The New York Times.

Previously, I was a staff writer at The Daily Standard, where I won an award for my investigative series on algorithmic bias.
